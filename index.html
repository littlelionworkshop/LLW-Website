About LLW

Thanks for stopping in!

Welcome to our tiny workshop! Victoria is a professionally trained costume designer and Curtis has a degree in electronics engineering. Together we’ve designed and built costumes and props for over 80 cosplays, and over 70 theatre and film shows since 2011.

In 2020, with help of family, friends and youtube we built a 28 ft tiny house, and have been crafting in it since. Today in our 240 sq ft home, we are focussing our skills on creating high-quality, custom-built, sewing-focussed cosplay commissions, and 3D printed small props, and accessories. We’re so grateful to have worked with so many incredibly kind, creative, authentic cosplayers around the world, and we would love to make your next project come to life!
